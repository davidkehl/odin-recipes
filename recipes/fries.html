Homemade French Fries

Description

This recipe features crispy, golden-brown French fries made from fresh potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or other seasonings

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a paper towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry the potato strips in batches until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove fries from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Season with salt and any additional spices while still hot.
