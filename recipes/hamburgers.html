Hamburgers

Description

This hamburger recipe features juicy beef patties, fresh toppings, and a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles for toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Form the mixture into 4 equal patties.
  4. Cook patties on the grill or skillet for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble burgers with your choice of toppings.
