Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
  4. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente; drain.
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and ½ teaspoon salt.
  8. Spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Layer 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.
  10. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  11. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices.
  12. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  13. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or use a piece of parchment paper under the foil.
  14. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
  16. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
